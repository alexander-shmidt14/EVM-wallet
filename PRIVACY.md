# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: August 26, 2025**

## Introduction

Smart Wallet ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our non-custodial cryptocurrency wallet application.

## Data We DO NOT Collect

- **Private Keys and Seed Phrases**: We never have access to your private keys, seed phrases, or wallet credentials. These are generated and stored locally on your device only.
- **Transaction Data**: We do not collect, store, or monitor your transaction history or wallet balances.
- **Personal Information**: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal identifying information.
- **Usage Analytics**: We do not track how you use the app or collect behavioral data.
- **Device Information**: We do not collect device identifiers, location data, or technical specifications.

## Data We May Process

- **Network Requests**: The app makes requests to Ethereum RPC providers (Alchemy/Infura) and blockchain explorers (Etherscan) to fetch blockchain data. These requests are made directly from your device.
- **Token Price Data**: The app fetches cryptocurrency price information from CoinGecko's public API.

## Local Data Storage

All sensitive data is stored exclusively on your device:

- **Seed Phrases**: Encrypted and stored in your device's secure storage (Android Keystore / Windows Credential Manager)
- **Transaction History**: Stored locally to provide transaction history
- **App Settings**: Stored locally for your convenience

## Third-Party Services

The app integrates with:

- **Ethereum RPC Providers** (Alchemy/Infura): For blockchain data
- **Etherscan API**: For transaction history (optional)
- **CoinGecko API**: For cryptocurrency prices

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them.

## Data Security

- All sensitive data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption
- Private keys never leave your device
- No data is transmitted to our servers (we don't have servers)
- You are fully responsible for backing up your seed phrase

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's no personal data to access, modify, or delete from our systems. All your data remains under your control on your device.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the app and on our website.
